API Endpoints

  • POST /simulator/watch
    Get current history_id
  • POST /simulator/subscribe
    Subscribe to webhook
  • GET /simulator/emails
    Get emails since history_id
  • GET /simulator/email/{id}
    Get email by ID
  • POST /simulator/send_email
    Send a new email
  • POST /simulator/classify
    Classify email content
